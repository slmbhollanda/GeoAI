# Why tungsten?

## What is it?
Tungsten (W) is a metal with the highest melting point: 3422°C.
US and EU list tungsten as a "critical mineral".

## Where I'm looking
Correas region, São Paulo state, Brazil.  
A known tin-tungsten deposit, poorly mapped with modern technology.

## What I'm building
A low-cost drone + AI system to find tungsten deposits.
