# GeoIA – Tungsten detection with drones + AI

**Sérgio, 12 yo** 

## What is this?
GeoIA is a low-cost system to detect scheelite (tungsten ore) using:
1. Satellite imagery + AI
2. A basic drone + UV light
3. Physical markers

## Why tungsten?
- Strategic metal (defense, electronics, drilling)
- Correas region (São Paulo state) has a known Sn-W deposit
